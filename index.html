Filipino Heritage

     

The Philippines is known for its rich history influenced by indigenous, Asian, and Western cultures. From ancient traditions to Spanish architecture, there’s much to explore.

   
   
     

Traditional Food

     
           
  • Adobo: A savory dish of marinated meat.
    •        
  • Sinigang: A sour soup with vegetables and meat or seafood.
    •        
  • Lechon: A roasted whole pig, often served during celebrations.

Festivals

     

Join us in celebrating festivals like Sinulog, Ati-Atihan, and Pahiyas, which showcase Filipino creativity, spirituality, and joy.
